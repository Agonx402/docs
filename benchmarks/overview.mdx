Agon publishes two benchmark families.

## Live Devnet Benchmarks

These come from the protocol-only devnet demo. They capture:

- scenario shape
- explorer links
- serialized transaction bytes where measured
- settlement compression against an exact-payment baseline

## Synthetic Capacity Benchmarks

These come from deterministic sizing scripts. They measure:

- unilateral message density
- bundle settlement limits
- cooperative clearing-round headroom
- projected BLS headroom

## Source Of Truth

The generated pages in this section are built from committed JSON snapshots rather than hand-maintained prose. That keeps the published tables tied to reproducible artifacts.
