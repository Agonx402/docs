Agon Protocol is a non-custodial clearing protocol for machine payments on Solana.

Agon lets agents and services make many payments first and settle them later. It is designed for repeated API usage, machine-to-machine commerce, and other payment flows where settling every interaction as its own transfer is too expensive.

## What Agon Includes

Agon V4 includes:

- permanent participant identities
- permanent one-way payment channels
- signed cumulative commitments
- direct settlement
- bundle settlement
- cooperative clearing rounds

## Common Use Cases

Agon is a good fit for:

- agent payments for APIs and infrastructure
- repeated buyer-to-provider payment flows
- payment hubs and operator services
- payment flows that need guaranteed or trust-minimized options
