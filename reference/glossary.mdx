---
title: "Glossary"
description: "Key terms used throughout the Agon Protocol docs."
---

- **Participant**: a permanent wallet-bound protocol identity with a stable `participant_id`
- **Channel**: a permanent one-way payment relationship keyed by `payer_id + payee_id + token_id`
- **Latest commitment**: the newest unilateral cumulative state for one channel
- **Bundle settlement**: several unilateral commitments for one payee settled in fewer transactions
- **Clearing round**: one shared signed message that advances several channels together
- **Gross routed value**: total value represented by all advanced unilateral obligations before netting
- **Residual net flow**: the final participant balance change that remains after cooperative offsetting
